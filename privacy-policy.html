Regulatory Compliance and Data Security

At ArchiHarmony, we are dedicated to adhering to all relevant legal frameworks, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Protecting your personal data and privacy is our top priority. We implement state-of-the-art security measures to ensure your information is secure. Our compliance strategy is designed to evolve with legislative changes, allowing us to maintain leadership in data protection standards.

We perform consistent audits and evaluations to assess our compliance status and pinpoint areas for enhancement. Our team is well-versed in the significance of data protection and actively promotes a privacy-centric culture within our organization. By partnering with legal professionals and data protection agencies, we aim to uphold the highest levels of compliance and ethical standards.
